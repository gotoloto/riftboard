Ranks each legend by how much it'd cost to finish building its top-25% composite deck (1 legend + 3 battlefields + 12 runes + 40 maindeck) from your current collection. Prices are TCGplayer market (USD) embedded on riftdecks card pages and cached in cards-catalog.json; cards with no listed price fall back to a rarity-weighted estimate (common $0.25, uncommon $0.75, rare $4, epic $30). Click a row to see exactly what's missing.
